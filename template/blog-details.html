Home for the Holidays: 7 Homes That Will Grant You the Perfect Christmas Snapshot

Build your own house and make sure it is Christmas-photo ready!

By Courtney Pittman

Friends, family, food (lots of food), and a cozy home are part of what make the holidays the most wonderful time of the year. The sound of wood crackling in the fireplace, the sweet smell of turkey roasting in the oven, and a laughter-filled family room deliver comfort, joy, and make us want to…well take photos of course (I mean, did it really happen if we don’t have picture proof?)! Capturing these moments and having memories to share with loved ones for years to come is part of what makes the holiday season so special.

Click here to browse our collection of home designs that feature two-stories, fireplaces, and open floor plans.

Features such as an open layout makes it easy for the whole family to squeeze into one photo without feeling cramped. Want to really set the holiday mood? A warm fireplace makes the perfect backdrop for those cozy pajama pictures (am I right?). Need more room to squeeze everyone in? Line family and friends up along a grand staircase for a dramatic photo. With thousands of Instagram-worthy home designs, you’re sure to find your dream holiday home.

This modern farmhouse design (plan 51-1145) is filled with features that are both luxurious and practical. Case in point: the master suite's enormous walk-in closet that opens directly to the laundry room. The vast great room features a fireplace and doors opening to both the front and rear porches – making it a great space for a photo op!

A handy mudroom just off the three-car garage keeps things tidy, while double pantries in the kitchen helps owners stay organized. A loft on the second level provides extra room during the holidays and can be used as a guest suite or game room.
